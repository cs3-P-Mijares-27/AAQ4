Astroinformatics 

The multidisciplinary area of "astroinformatics" brings together data science, statistics, computer science, and astronomy to handle, evaluate, and interpret enormous volumes of astronomical data. The area has emerged in reaction to the exponential growth of data produced by modern astronomical instruments, such as telescopes, satellites, and observatories, which can create terabytes of data each day.
